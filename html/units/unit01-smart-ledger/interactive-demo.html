üéØ Multi-Part Business Challenge

Scenario: You're helping a small business analyze their performance.

Step 1: Data Entry & Analysis

Use the spreadsheet above to enter this month's data:

  • Week 1 Sales: $2,500
  • Week 2 Sales: $3,200
  • Week 3 Sales: $2,800
  • Week 4 Sales: $3,500

Calculate the total and average using SUM and AVERAGE functions.

Step 2: Financial Planning

Use the NPV calculator to evaluate an expansion opportunity:

  • Initial Investment: $15,000
  • Year 1 Cash Flow: $5,000
  • Year 2 Cash Flow: $6,000
  • Year 3 Cash Flow: $7,000
  • Discount Rate: 8%

Step 3: Break-Even Analysis

Calculate the break-even point for a new product:

  • Fixed Costs: $8,000
  • Selling Price: $50
  • Variable Cost: $30

Step 4: Accounting Practice

Use the drag-and-drop exercise to record the journal entry for the first week's sales.

Step 5: Business Simulation

Apply your knowledge in the lemonade stand simulation and try to achieve a $20 profit!
