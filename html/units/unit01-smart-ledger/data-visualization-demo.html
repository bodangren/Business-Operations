Understanding Break-Even Charts

The break-even chart shows two lines:

  • Revenue Line (Blue): Shows total revenue at different sales volumes
  • Total Cost Line (Red): Shows fixed costs plus variable costs

Where these lines intersect is your break-even point - the number of units you need to sell to cover all costs.
