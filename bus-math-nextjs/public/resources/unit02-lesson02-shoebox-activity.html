Shoebox Receipt Challenge

Unit 02 · Lesson 02 · Phase 1

Challenge Steps

  1. Cut the receipts apart after printing, then shuffle them into a "shoebox" pile.
  2. Sort each receipt into the category you think is most accurate. Flag anything you are unsure about.
  3. Enter each transaction into the Month-End Ledger template. Document your assumptions in the notes column.
  4. Compare totals with your partner team and discuss any mismatches or ambiguous receipts.

Ledger Categories To Use

Sort the receipts into these buckets before entering the data:

  • Office & Supplies
  • Utilities & Services
  • Client Meals & Events
  • Travel & Transportation
  • Miscellaneous / Needs Clarification
