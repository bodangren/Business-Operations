# Product Definition

## Project Name
Math for Business Operations: Applied Accounting with Excel

## Target Users
- Grade 12 Business Operations students
- Teachers delivering accounting and business math curriculum
- Students preparing for post-secondary business programs

## Educational Goals
- Master fundamental accounting principles (debits, credits, T-accounts, journal entries)
- Develop Excel proficiency for business applications (formulas, functions, automation)
- Build financial literacy through authentic business scenarios
- Prepare investor-ready financial models and presentations
- Develop Courage, Adaptability, and Persistence (CAP) through project-based learning

## Key Features
1. **8 Educational Units** (2 weeks each) + 13-week Capstone Project
2. **Interactive Learning Components**
   - T-accounts and journal entry builders
   - Drag-and-drop exercises
   - Comprehension checks and quizzes
   - Reflection journals
   - Business simulation scenarios
3. **Excel Integration**
   - Spreadsheet components
   - Financial dashboards
   - Practice datasets (CSV)
4. **Teacher Resources**
   - Lesson plans
   - Classroom routines
   - Assessment rubrics
5. **Student Pathways**
   - Unit introduction pages
   - 6-phase lesson structure
   - Progress tracking

## Deliverables
- Complete 8-unit curriculum with 10 lessons per unit
- Interactive React components for formative assessment
- Excel practice files and solutions
- Teacher lesson plans and assessment tools
- Capstone project guidelines and rubrics

## Audience for Final Work
- Real investors, entrepreneurs, and business professionals
- Post-secondary business program admissions
- Industry mentors and community partners
